About Habesha Pizza

Habesha Pizza was established on April 19, 2007. Our first house was located in Ethiopia, Addis Ababa.

Why Habesha?

Habesha (ሐበሻ) is the name give for Ethiopian people. Since we first launched in Ethiopia, we named our Pizza house the HABESHA PIZZA HOUSE.

Habesha Pizza has it's pizza houses in Ethiopia, Kenya, South-Africa and Egypt.

Now, we have started an online ordering and delivery.
